Privacy Policy

How Pure Taste Table collects, uses and protects your information.

Last updated: 16 July 2026

At Pure Taste Table ("we", "us", "our"), accessible from https://www.puretastetable.com, the privacy of our visitors is a priority we take seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices and rights available to you. By using this website, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who We Are

Pure Taste Table is a recipe and home-cooking publication. If you have any questions about this policy or your personal data, contact us at hello@puretastetable.com or through our Contact page.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide voluntarily

We only collect personal information that you actively choose to share with us, for example when you:

  • Contact us by email or through our contact page (name, email address, and the contents of your message);
  • Request a recipe, submit feedback, or report a correction.

Transparency about how your data is used: any information you submit is used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. If we ever wish to use your information for any additional purpose — such as a newsletter — we will ask for your clear, separate, opt-in consent first, and you may withdraw that consent at any time.

2.2 Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we automatically receive certain technical information when you visit, including your IP address (truncated/anonymised where supported), browser type, device type, referring pages, pages viewed, and the date and time of your visit. This data is aggregated and used only to understand how the site is used and to improve it.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site (for example, which recipes are most popular). Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information such as pages visited, time on site, and approximate geographic region. This information is aggregated and does not directly identify you.

  • Learn how Google uses data from sites that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • You can opt out of Google Analytics at any time using the official browser add-on: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

4. Advertising, Google Ads & Third-Party Cookies

We may display advertising on this website, including advertising served through Google's advertising services (such as Google AdSense or campaigns run through Google Ads). In connection with such advertising:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies (such as the Google advertising cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of many third-party vendors' cookies at www.aboutads.info/choices.

Our advertising commitments: we do not permit ads on this site that misrepresent who we are, disguise themselves as content, or collect your personal information without disclosure. Any sponsored or paid content is clearly labelled. We comply with Google Ads policies on transparency and honest representation in all promotional activity related to this website.

5. Lead Generation & Phone Contact Transparency

In line with Google Ads policies on lead generation and call-based advertising, we make the following commitments:

  • We clearly state, at the point of collection, why we are asking for any piece of personal information and what it will be used for.
  • We never collect personal information under false pretences, and we never share leads or enquiries with undisclosed third parties.
  • If we ever publish a phone number or run call-based (including call-only) advertising campaigns, calls will connect to us directly, the business name presented in the ad will match this website, and calls may only be recorded with clear notice to you at the start of the call and only where permitted by law.
  • We do not use misleading claims, fake urgency, or unclear pricing in any advertisement or landing page associated with this website.

6. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them for analytics and, where applicable, advertising as described above. For a complete explanation of the cookies used on this site and how to control them, please read our dedicated Cookies Policy.

7. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information we hold, including serving this website over HTTPS and limiting access to correspondence to the people who need it to respond. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We keep correspondence only as long as needed to handle your enquiry and for a reasonable period afterwards for record-keeping, after which it is deleted. Aggregated analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' standard retention settings.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
  • The right to correct inaccurate data;
  • The right to request deletion of your data;
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing;
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@puretastetable.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.

10. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the age of digital consent in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external websites we do not operate. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be highlighted on the site.

13. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email hello@puretastetable.com or use our Contact page.
